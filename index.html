Introduction

Switzerland is one of the most breathtaking countries in central Europe, the mountainous terrain and rolling hills give a great backdrop to this beautiful country. With great places to visit such as The Jungfrau Region, Bern and The Ice Caves it is no wonder that this country is shrouded with beauty.

Natural beauty and wildlife are key parts of Switzerland and this picture clearly shows that there is both big and small parts too the beauty of the landscape.

Travelling around the mountainous terrain can be a challenge so using the train routes and other transport will greatly improve the experience. It will also give a vast range of views while travelling around.

Switzerlands natural beauty is not always visible, the hidden parts are just as stunning but maybe even more. Areas like the Ice Caves are great examples of this and really let visitors experience the scenery and walk through time.

After experiencing some of the activities and views there are plenty of places that can be visited, these locations provide scenic surroundings and a great space to sit and admire the natural beauty of the country.

All around Switzerland are rivers and these become even more apparent when visiting the towns and citys. Some of these water bodies have activities to take part in such as boat trips so make perfect places to go and visit for a quiet time.
